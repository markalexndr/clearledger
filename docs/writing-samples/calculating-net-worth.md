# Calculating Net Worth

Net worth is a simple financial indicator used to understand an individual's overall financial position.

It compares the value of assets with the total value of debts.

---

## Net Worth Formula

Net Worth = Assets – Debts

Assets represent financial resources owned by an individual.

Examples include:

- savings
- investments
- cash balances

Debts represent financial obligations.

Examples include:

- credit card balances
- loans
- overdrafts

---

## Example

Assets: £25,000  
Debts: £18,000  

Net Worth = £7,000

This indicates that total assets exceed liabilities.

---

## Why Net Worth Matters

Net worth provides a clearer financial picture than income alone.

Two individuals with identical incomes may have very different financial positions depending on their debts and assets.

For this reason, net worth is often used as a simple long-term financial indicator.
